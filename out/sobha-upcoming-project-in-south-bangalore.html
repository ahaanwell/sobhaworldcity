Sobha Upcoming Project in South Bangalore

South Bangalore has always been one of the best places to live in the city because it has good infrastructure, lots of social amenities and is close to major IT job centers. Bannerghatta Road, Hosur Road, Electronic City, Banashankari and Kanakapura Road are still popular with buyers who want to be connected and have easy access to things they need every day. Because of this, homebuyers and investors who trust the Sobha brand are naturally very interested in any upcoming Sobha project in South Bangalore.

Upcoming Projects from Sobha in South Bangalore

Sobha Limited has planned and announced several high end residential developments in South Bangalore. These developments are mainly for people who want to live in well developed areas with high quality homes. Some of the most important new Sobha projects in this area are Sobha Magnus on Bannerghatta Road and Sobha Townpark near Hosur Road and Attibele.

Sobha Magnus is a high end residential project that offers large 3 BHK and 4 BHK apartments with modern amenities and high end features. Because it is on Bannerghatta Road, it is easy to get to schools, hospitals and shopping areas.

Sobha Townpark, which is close to Electronic City and Hosur Road, is planned to be a large residential development that focuses on lifestyle living. The project aims to meet the needs of families working in South Bangalore's IT corridor by building several residential towers, landscaped open spaces and community amenities.

Things that make South Bangalore not a good place for long term investors

South Bangalore is a stable and convenient place to live, but its real estate market is also fairly mature. There isn't much land available, project sizes are often limited and property prices are already high. This could lower the potential for long term appreciation, especially for investors who want to see more growth over the next ten years.

Because of these things, a lot of people who were originally looking for a Sobha upcoming project in South Bangalore are now looking at new areas with more potential for growth in the future.

What Makes Sobha World City Different

If you're looking for something other than saturated markets, Sobha World City in East Bangalore is a great option. Sobha World City was meant to be a big, connected community with lots of open space, modern infrastructure, lifestyle amenities and the ability to grow over time. This is hard to find in South Bangalore.

East Bangalore is still seeing benefits from more infrastructure, better connections and more business activity. These things make Sobha World City a great place for investors who want to see their money grow and for families who want to live in a well planned township.

Conclusion

Long term investors are becoming more interested in developments that are ready for the future, while buyers looking for established areas are interested in Sobha's upcoming projects in South Bangalore, such as Sobha Magnus and Sobha Townpark. Sobha World City has the same high quality construction that Sobha is known for, as well as planning for a whole town and a lot of room for growth. For buyers who want a good mix of lifestyle, space and investment value, Sobha World City is a better and more forward thinking choice.
